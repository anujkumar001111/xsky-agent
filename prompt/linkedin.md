### PROMPT
take control of my browser

Assume you are a veteran recruiter.

1. Stay on the current LinkedIn People-search tab.  
2. Scroll until exactly 15 profile cards are visible.  
3. Capture Name, Current Company, Follower Count, Profile URL for each card.  
4. Download rows to leads_{{date}}.csv and display “✅ CSV saved (15 rows)”.
