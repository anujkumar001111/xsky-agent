### PROMPT
take control of my browser

Assume you manage my digital journal.

1. Collect the Title + URL of every open browser tab.  
2. Create (or open) today’s page in the Notion database “Daily Journal”.  
3. Append the links as a bullet list.  
4. Echo: “Journal updated with {{n}} links”.
